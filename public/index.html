Rules of Engagement

1
Teams must register before the specified deadline to participate in the event.
2
Each team must consist of the approved number of members; no changes allowed once the event begins.
3
Participants are allowed to be part of only one team.
4
Strict adherence to instructions provided by the organizers throughout the event.
5
Only components purchased through the official points-based system may be used for building.
6
Use of external or pre-owned components is strictly prohibited.
7
Teams must manage their allocated points efficiently; overspending may impact evaluation.
8
Exchange or sharing of components between teams is not allowed under any circumstances.
9
Any damage caused to components or equipment due to negligence may result in penalties.
10
Participants must handle all materials responsibly and return unused components.
11
Completion of prototype must be within the allocated time limits for each round.
12
Maintain discipline and follow all safety guidelines during the entire event.
13
The decisions made by the judges and organizing committee will be final and binding.
14
Organizers reserve the right to modify rules to ensure smooth execution of the event.
